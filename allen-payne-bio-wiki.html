    Who is Allen Payne?

    New York-born Allen Payne is an actor. He is active in the acting field since 1989. Presently, he is a prominent figure among the media for starring in Tyler Perry’s sitcom, “House of Payne”.

    He played the character of C.J. Payne in the series from 2006 to 2012. Additionally, he is famous for starring in the American crime film, “New Jack City”.

    Allen Payne began his acting career in 1989. He made his film debut in 1989. It was the crime/dance drama film named “Rooftops”. Afterward, in 1991, he featured in the crime film, “New Jack City” alongside Wesley Snipes.

    This role made him popular and famous. He still kept on working hard. Consequently, this made him a better actor ever since.

    Additionally, he played in movies like “The Walking Dead”, “Double Platinum”, “The Perfect Storm”, and others.

    Contrary to films, he made his TV debut in the same year 1990. He appeared on the TV show, “The Cosby Show”. Allen portrayed the role of Lance Rodman from 1990 to 1992.

    But his career breakthrough role came in another sitcom named, “House of Payne” in 2006. He portrayed the character of C.J. Payne in the sitcom from 2006 to 2012.
