    Corey Brown Obituary, Death – South Carolina’s North Charleston Hospital was the location where Corey Thomas Brown, a devoted husband and father, went away at the age of 33. Scott and Leesa Woodson Brown welcomed their son Corey into the world on October 4, 1989 in the town of Supply, North Carolina. In the year 2010, he relocated to Jamestown, South Carolina, and he has made it his permanent residence.

    Additionally, Corey was a leading member of The Santee River Rats yacht club in addition to working as a technician for Autoworks of Charleston. When he had free time, he would spend it with his son, teaching him new things and having fun with life. on his spare time, Corey liked activities such as golfing, fishing, riding a four-wheeler, playing pool, and navigating the Santee River on his jet boat. When it came to his family and friends, he placed a strong emphasis on “making memories baybeee!”

    Corey has a disposition that was both outgoing and free-spirited. His chuckle was loud and infectious to say the least. In addition to having a big heart, he liked everyone. If Corey ever encountered a stranger, he never failed to leave an impression on them. Both Corey’s wife, Kacie Casselman Brown, who resides in Jamestown, South Carolina, and their son, Garrett, are still alive. Parents, Larry and Ricki Brown, who reside in Supply, North Carolina.

    The parents are Leesa Woodson Brown of Loris, South Carolina, and Scott Brown of Supply, North Carolina. Chelsea Brown, also known as tuts, her fiance Hunter Hewett of North Carolina, and their children Kayleigh and Wyatt as well as their sister Chelsea Brown.
