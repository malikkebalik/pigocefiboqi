    What is the relationship of Andrew Catalon?

    Andrew Catalon’s relationship status is married.

    He is married to Jessica Layton. Jessica is a NewsChannel 13 anchor and reporter. She grew up in Burnt Hills and is a part of a singing cabaret group called Singing Anchors.

    Their relationship started when he sent an e-mail to her welcoming her to the News Channel 13 family. Eventually, their relationship grew as they had respect for each other’s passion for their career.

    Andrew used to cover the football games of Jessica. On 4th July 2010, Andrew proposed Jessica which she accepted and got engaged.

    They married on 17th July 2010 at St. Pius X Church in Loudonville with a reception at Saratoga National.

    Together they have a son Christopher James Catalon. They reside in Albany, N.Y.

    Who is Andrew Catalon?

    Andrew Catalon is a professional Sportscaster. Andrew is well-known for his work in CBS Network.

    Currently, he is working for CBS Network as their play-by-play announcer.

    Recently, on 15th August 2019, Buffalo Bills shared a video of Andrew previewing Bills-Panthers.

    Andrew Catalon: Early Life

    Andrew Catalon was born in Short Hills, N.J in 1980.

    He graduated from Syracuse University in Syracuse, New York.

    Andrew Catalon: Professional Career

    In the year 2016, he worked as a tennis announcer for the Olympic Games. He was a play-by-play commentator at the Sochi Olympic Winter Games.

    At present, he is working as a play-by-play announcer for the NCAA basketball, golf, and NFL coverage of CBS Sports.

    Before that. he called both the CBS and the US Masters and PGA golf championships, CBS Sports Network Open Tennis.

    Before CBS

    Similarly, he worked alongside Jamie Erdahl when he covered Georgia State vs Cincinnati game.
