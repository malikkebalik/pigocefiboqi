    Daniel Day-Lewis Education

    Daniel was born and raised in Kensington, London before his parents moved. There he attended Invicta and Sherington Primary Schools. He had to deal with the environment where the South London Children were very tough.

    There he was bullied for being Jewish and ”Posh” and his parents had to send him to the independent Sevenoaks School in Kent in 1968.

    After two years he was transferred to another independent school, Bedales in Petersfield, Hampshire. His sister was studying there. He also attended Bristol Old Vic Theatre School where he studied for a period of three years alongside Miranda Richardson.

    Daniel starred in the 2007 American epic period drama film, There Will Be Blood. The film was written and directed by Paul Thomas Anderson. It was based in the 1927 novel Oil! by Upton Sinclair.

He is portrayed as a silver miner-turned-oilman on a ruthless quest for wealth during Southern California’s oil boom of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. The film was released on September 27, 2007 (Fantastic Fest).
