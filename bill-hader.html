    William Thomas Hader, better known as Bill Hader, is a multi-talented American actor, comedian, writer, filmmaker, director, and producer. He has appeared as a supporting actor in a variety of episodes and films, including his breakout performance in the Dark series Barry, for which he received an Emmy nomination. He has appeared in films such as Tropic Thunder, Men in Black, The Anger Management, Turbo, Monsters University, and others. His main career began in 1999 and has grown steadily since then due to his hard work and dedication.

    Bill Hader- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings

    On June 7, 1978, the remarkable Bill Hader was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma. He was the first child of William Thomas Hader and Sherri Reece. He was given the name William Thomas Hader Jr. His father owns an air cargo company and also works as a restaurant manager and truck driver. His father, like Bill, was a stand-up comedian on the side. Sherri, his mother, was a dance teacher. He has two sisters, Katie and Kara.

    Education

    Patrick Henry Elementary School was where he finished his education. As he grew older, he attended Edison Junior High School before enrolling at Cascia Hall Preparatory School, where he earned his high school diploma.

    Bill Hader- Relationship, Married Life

    After co-starring in the film The To-Do List with Rachel Bilson, Hader began dating her in 2019. The couple made their debut appearance at the 77th Golden Globe Awards in 2020, but it was announced in July 2020 that they had split up.

    Hader married Maggie Carey, a writer, and filmmaker, in 2006. Their three daughters are Hannah, Harper, and Hayley. Hader filed for divorce in December 2017, following the couple’s breakup announcement in November 2017. The uncontested divorce was negotiated in March 2018, and it was finalized three months later.

    There are reports that the actor is dating Anna Kendrick in late 2020 or early 2021. Someone revealed that the couple has been dating quietly for a year. They both appeared as co-stars in the Disney + Christmas feature Noelle.
