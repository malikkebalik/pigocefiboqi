    Who is Heaven Hart, Kevin Hart’s daughter? Wiki Bio

    Heaven Hart was born in Santa Barbara, California USA, on 22 March 2005, so her zodiac sign is Aries and she holds American nationality. She is a TV celebrity, but best known for being the daughter of Kevin Hart, a famous American actor and comedian, and her mother Torrei Hart who is also an actress.

    Childhood and education

    Heaven spent the first five years with her parents, before they parents decided to split in February 2010, filing for divorce citing ‘irreconcilable differences’.

    Heaven has a brother named Hendrix, and both Kevin and Torrei agreed on joint custody of their two children – the divorce was finalized in November 2011.

    Heaven began attending elementary school, but spent a lot of her time travelling between her parents’ houses. On 18 August 2014, her father proposed to Eniko Parrish, marrying her on 13 August 2016. Heaven seems to be in good relations with her stepmother, as Eniko occasionally uploads pictures with her onto her Instagram account. In 2017, Eniko gave birth to Heaven’s half-brother named Kenzo Kash Hart, although on 15 December 2017, Kevin admitted that he cheated on Eniko while she was pregnant – nevertheless, the two are still together.

    Is Heaven going to become an actress?

    Heaven has been taking acting lessons since she was eight years old, and has featured in two of the movies which her father has appeared in, however, she doesn’t appear to be very interested in acting, and is rather considering a career in medicine, hoping to become a nurse in the future.

    Family, friends, love life and relationships

    Heaven seems too young to be dating, and there have been no rumors regarding any boys whom she might have dated. She was seen spending her free time with a mysterious boy on two occasions, while it was later reporter that this is only her friend from school, and that they are often studying together.

    Heaven is a highly sociable girl who enjoys spending time with her brother and her half-brother. She often acts as their big sister, taking care of them, giving them sweets and preparing food, while she also has many friends at school who have described her as humorous, friendly and outgoing. She is also very close to her mother, who has named her business called Heavenly Hart Hair Care after her daughter. The two are spending a lot of their free time together, and Heaven has occasionally kept her mother company during the filming of her movies and TV series.

    Hobbies and other interests

    Heaven is a huge fan of the film industry, with her favorite actors and actresses being Tom Hanks, Bruce Willis and Cate Blanchett, while some of her favorite movies include “Catch Me If You Can”, “The Aviator”, and “The Fifth Element”.

    She likes to listen to music too, especially to today’s popular singers such as Ed Sheeran, Justin Bieber, and Lady Gaga, while some of her favorite songs are “Shape of You”, “Baby”, and “Shallow” which is the soundtrack of the “A Star is Born” film that she enjoys watching.

    Heaven likes being outdoors rather than stuck between four walls, and enjoys spending her time in nature surrounded by trees and mountains. She was a girl scout when she was young, and often camped with her friends learning how to set up tents and make knots with ropes. Heaven is a lover of animals too, and has a pet dog and a pet cat.

    Appearance and net worth

    Heaven is 14 years old. She has long brown hair and brown eyes, is 5ft 2ins (1.58m) tall, and weighs around 106lbs (48kgs). As of early 2020, her father Kevin’s net worth is estimated at over $200 million; her mother’s is unknown.

    Who is Kevin Hart?

    Kevin Hart was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania USA, on 6 July 1979 – his zodiac sign is Cancer and he holds American nationality. He is a stand-up comedian, producer and an actor, best known for his roles in movies such as “Paper Soldiers” released in 2002 and “Scary Movie” shot in 2003. Kevin was raised solely by his mother, Nancy Hart who worked at the University of Pennsylvania as a systems analyst, as his father Henry Witherspoon was addicted to cocaine, and spent a lot of time behind bars.

    Kevin has an older brother named Robert, and the two found comfort in comedy when having to cope with their family situation. Kevin attended George Washington High School, and after matriculation moved to Brockton in Massachusetts, finding a job and not enrolling at college.

    He started working as a shoe salesman, while occasionally performing in stand-up comedy clubs around Brockton. His first major performance was at the Laff House in Philadelphia, which wasn’t that good as he was booed, however, he worked on his material and next time made the entire audience laugh.

    He has since been on several comedy tours, making $15 million from “Laugh at My Pain” tour which he undertook in 2009. His latest comedy tour began on 9 April 2015 called “What Now? Tour”, which ended on 7 August 2016 after nearly a year and a half of travelling all around the world.

    He made his debut film appearance in “Paper Soldiers” in 2002, while he became famous after starring in movies such as “Scary Movie”, “Soul Plane”, and “Death at a Funeral”. He appeared in many movies and also made occasional TV series appearances between 2002 and 2015, in which year he starred in two movies – “Get Hard” and “The Wedding Ringer”, and the next year in “Central Intelligence” and “The Secret Life of Pets” films.

    He became famous worldwide after his role in “Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle” in 2017, and then produced his first film in 2018 entitled “Night School”. He reprised his role in “Jumanji: The Next Level” in 2019, while he has recently been cast to appear in the film “Fatherhood”, set to be released in 2021.

    Kevin was arrested on 14 April 2013 after he nearly hit a tanker truck, with the suspicion that he was driving while intoxicated, and was sentenced to three years probation. He was involved in a car accident on 1 September 2019, when a car he was riding in crashed near Calabasas in California – Kevin was taken to a hospital and was released 10 days later.
