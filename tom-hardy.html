    Tom Hardy is a well-known British actor, producer, and model who has worked in the entertainment industry for many years. He is one of the most well-known British actors in the world, and he is adored and respected by both the industry and his fans.

    What is the Net Worth of Tom Hardy? Salary, Earnings

    Tom Hardy has been a member of the entertainment industry for numerous years and has worked in a variety of films and television series, through which he has amassed a fortune. His acting career provides the majority of his income, and his net worth is projected to be approximately $10 million as of 2022.

    Tom Hardy- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

    Tom Hardy was born on September 15, 1977, in Hammersmith, London, England, to Anne Barrett and Chips Hardy. His mother was a painter and artist, and his father was a novelist and writer. He is of mixed race because his mother is Irish and his father is English. Tom finished high school at Tower House School and Reeds School in London, as well as Duff Miller Sixth Form College. He studied acting at the Drama Centre London and the Richmond Drama School.

    Tom Hardy- Professional Career

    Tom began his modeling career after winning the Big Breakfast Find Me A Supermodel competition in 1998. He then began working with Models 1. He then enrolled in the Drama Center London to hone his acting abilities. In 2001, he made his cinematic debut in Hawk Down, and the following year, he played Jean Luc Picard in Star Trek: Nemesis, followed by roles in Dot the I and The Reckoning. Tom has appeared in various films, including LD 50 Lethal Dose in 2003, Marie Antoinette in 2006, Flood in 2007, Bronson in 2008, and Thick as Thieves in 2009.

    In 2010, he was cast as Eames in Christopher Nolan’s immensely popular film Inception, and he has since gone on to star in films such as This Means War (2012), The Dark Knight Rises (2012), Mad Max: Fury Road (2015), The Revenant (2015), and Dunkirk (2017). He also appeared in various television shows, including The Virgin Queen in 2005 as Robert Dudley and Wuthering Heights in 2008 as Heathcliff, when he met his wife. Aside from that, he has been in A for Andromeda (2006), Oliver Twist (2007), Stuart: A Life Backward (2007), The Take 2009, Peaky Blinders (2014), and numerous other television episodes.
