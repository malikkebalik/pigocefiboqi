    NEW YORK CITY, NEW YORK: Get ready for the next chapter in the love stories you adore! We TV has just announced that the fan-favorite show 'Life After Lockup' will be making a comeback with Season 6, which will have 10 new episodes airing from Friday, December 1 at 9 pm ET/8 pm CT.

    But that's not all – you can also catch the episodes on the AMC Networks streaming service, ALLBLK, every Tuesday.

    The excitement continues as your favorite couples navigate life outside the prison walls. Mark your calendars for the drama, the love, and the unexpected twists coming your way.

    But what is the fate of the couples' love lives? After navigating multiple hardships, are they still going strong? 

    'Life After Lockup' Season 6 couples now

    Britney and Kerok 

    Also Read: Are Choi Min-woo and Yoo Si-eun still together? 'Single's Inferno' Season 3 couple's social media hint at relationship status

    Love After Lockup's dynamic duo Britney and Kerok are still together. They first crossed paths while serving time at the same female facility for robbery. Kerok, who is a transgender man, initiated his journey of transition while behind bars and envisioned starting a family with Britney once they were both free.

    However, the plot took a turn when Kerok discovered from a doctor that pursuing IVF with Britney would require him to halt hormone replacement therapy. This revelation led to a shift in Kerok's perspective on having children.

    Also Read: Are Lee Gwan-Hee and Choi Hye-Seon still together? 'Single's Inferno' couple found their way back to each other in Netflix show

    Despite their journey together, Britney and Kerok find themselves at a crossroads when it comes to the topic of children. When Britney brought up the possibility of opting for IVF, Kerok staunchly declared, "I don't wanna talk about kids. Period," per Distractify.

    Melissa and Louie

    Love After Lockup's Melissa Picariello and Louie Fojut are facing more than just relationship hurdles – they're caught in the midst of an ongoing drama involving Louie's mother, Donna. The tension between Melissa and Donna persists as both women passionately believe they know what's best for Louie.

    Melissa's journey with Louie goes way back, with a high school crush that has stood the test of time. Despite the ongoing family drama, their love story remains resilient and continues to flourish.

    Joynomi and Redd

    'Love After Lockup' fame Joynomi has been in a long-distance relationship with her boyfriend Redd, who is currently behind bars, for a whopping seven years. Their love story began online, and interestingly, they've never met face-to-face.

    However, Joynomi confessed to a rocky chapter in their relationship.

    Four years into their connection, loneliness got the better of her, and she ended up cheating while Redd was in solitary confinement for six months. The plot thickened as Joynomi revealed that the unexpected consequence of her indiscretion was a pregnancy.

    Strikingly, she never informed the other man involved about their son, and she is now raising Swaee on her own.

    As their relationship progresses, Joynomi grapples with worries about Redd's reaction when they finally meet in person. The love story of this couple looks a bit concerning. 

    Shavel and Quaylon

    Quaylon Adams and Shavel Moore's relationship in 'Life After Lockup' took a rocky turn when Shavel discovered suggestive messages from other women on Quaylon's phone. Hurt and betrayed, Shavel promptly kicked him out of their shared home.

    Initially, Quaylon was done with the relationship, but a change of heart led him to want to win her back.

    Now, faced with trust issues and the aftermath of the phone discovery, Shavel is unsure if she can trust Quaylon again. The drama unfolds as Quaylon, without the chance to explain his side of the story, heads back to Houston.

    The current status of their relationship hangs in the balance, promising more twists and turns in the We TV duo's journey on 'Life After Lockup'. 

    Justine and Michael

    Justine and Michael's love story has been a wild ride from the start, keeping fans on the edge of their seats as they weathered the storms of their relationship. Despite the ups and downs, it looks like this dynamic duo has managed to work through their issues and is still going strong.

    The latest twist in their tale? Justine is expecting a baby with Michael. While some viewers have criticized their relationship, accusing them of having ulterior motives, Justine and Michael have stood firm in their commitment to each other.

    Their love has weathered the storms of skepticism, proving that they're in it for the long haul as they navigate the path of parenthood together now. 

    Blaine and Lindsey 

    Blaine and Lindsey's love story is standing the test of time. Blaine found himself catching feelings for Lindsey, his long-time friend, which then turned into a deeper connection between the two.

    Get ready for a tale of friendship turning into something more as Blaine and Lindsey navigate the twists and turns of their evolving relationship in the new season.

    Tayler and Chance

    Chance and Tayler's love story, born on the show 'Love After Lockup', faced its fair share of challenges, all documented on the spin-off 'Life After Lockup'. Doubts rose among fans about the couple's compatibility, but Chance remained unwavering in his dedication to making things work for the one he loves.

    Even when the cameras stopped rolling, the couple found a way to stay together, evident from Tayler's updates about her everyday life shared on social media. 

    Shawn and Sara

    Sara and Shawn are still holding on — mostly. In a November 2022 Instagram post, the 'Life After Lockup' star opened up about her current situation with her partner.

    "Yes, I'm still with my husband," Sara shared. "Married life is a whole different ball game. Like any relationship, it has its highs and lows. It's pretty much like any regular relationship."

    However, she quickly acknowledged that their love story has been far from a smooth ride.

    "Everything with Shawn and me happened so fast. I jumped into a serious relationship without knowing if it would even work out," the 30-year-old confessed. Get ready for a whirlwind of emotions as Sara and Shawn navigate the challenges of their fast-paced romance.

    Aris and Cameron

    'Love After Lockup' spinoff stars Aris and Cameron Morton are still going strong even after filming the recent season of the reality TV series.

    The couple has reached a new milestone in their journey as they welcome their first child together, a daughter named Charlie!
