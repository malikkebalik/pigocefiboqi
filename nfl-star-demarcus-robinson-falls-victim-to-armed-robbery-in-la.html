    In a troubling incident, Demarcus Robinson, a receiver for the Rams, was robbed at gunpoint in Los Angeles on Friday morning.

    According to TMZ Sports, the police responded to reports of a robbery outside a hotel in Los Angeles just after midnight. Two armed men approached the 29-year-old football player and demanded his valuables, including a high-value watch.

    Robinson complied with the demands, and the perpetrators fled the scene. Fortunately, the wideout reportedly remains unharmed in spite of the distressing encounter. The authorities have initiated an investigation into the incident, but no arrests have been made so far.

    Having commenced his career with the Kansas City Chiefs in 2016 and contributing to the victory of Patrick Mahomes in Super Bowl LIV in 2020, Robinson transitioned to the Rams this season. However, he has yet to make a reception in five games.
