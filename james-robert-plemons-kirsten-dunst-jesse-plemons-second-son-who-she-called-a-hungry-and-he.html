    James Robert Plemons is actress Kristen Dunst's son with actor Jesse Plemenos. Although their child is shielded from the media, the "Spider-Man" star once talked about James Robert, calling him a heavy baby.

    By the time Kristen Dunst and Jesse Plemons tied the knot, they were already parents to two beautiful children, Ennis Howard Plemons and James Robert Plemons.

    The couple got an early start to their careers and became child stars. In 1994, Dunst landed a Golden Globe nomination for her role in "Interview with Vampire: The Vampire Chronicles," alongside A-listers Tom Cruise and Brad Pitt.

    Kirsten Dunst and Jesse Plemons at the Directors Guild of America on April 26, 2023, in Los Angeles, California.

    The following year, she landed a lead role in "Jumanji," alongside Robert Williams. Her other box office hits include "Little Women" and "Small Soldiers."

    However, one of her most recognized characters is Mary Jane Watson in the original "Spider-Man" film series, alongside her leading man Tobey Maguire. Other "Spider-Man" films were also released as a part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, which stars Andrew Garfield and Tom Holland.

    The couple, who tied the knot in July the year before, stepped out to have dinner with their boys. As seen in paparazzi photos, both boys were blonde.

    Plemons' career was no less of a success. His role as Landry Clarke in "Friday Night Lights" catapulted him into stardom. Plemons' talent as an actor has been recognized several times through Emmy Award, Academy Award, BAFTA Award, and Independent Spirit Award nominations.

    Dunst and Plemons met through their work in 2015 when their names were already known in the entertainment industry. They got engaged two years later and welcomed their first child, Ennis, in 2018. In 2021, they welcomed their second son, James Robert, and tied the knot in Jamaica soon after.

    Kirsten Dunst Called Her Son an 'Angel'

    Dunst steers clear of social media, so she announced her first pregnancy through Rodarte's fall 2018 lookbook while wearing pieces from the brand. She said of the shoot:

    "Every shot was on the floor. I was like, 'I can't get up.' I felt Urkel."

    Then expecting, Dunst looked stunning in an off-white lace dress emphasizing her growing belly. She also donned a lavender sheer dress and a sparkling hot pink gown, among others. The actress undoubtedly looked breathtaking in every photo, showing the beginning of a new chapter in her life.

    When Jesse Plemons' son, James Robert, was born, Dunst also shared a glimpse of their son, who weighed 18 pounds four months after his birth. "He's an angel, but he's a hungry angel and a heavy angel," she told the New York Times in an interview.

    Dunst opened up about becoming a second-time mom to a baby, revealing she hasn't slept through the night since James Robert was born. At that time, Dunst embraced motherhood at home while her husband worked, filming for the drama "Killers of the Flower Moon."

    What Does James Robert Plemons Look Like Now?

    Kristen Dunst and Jesse Plemons' children are kept from the public eye. The couple refrains from sharing photos of the kids online. However, their family was spotted out and about in 2022, strolling in Los Angeles.
