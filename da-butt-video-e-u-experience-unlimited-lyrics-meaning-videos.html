    Overall Meaning

    The song "Da Butt" by Experience Unlimited is a classic funk and R&B hit that was released in 1988. The lyrics of the song tell the story of a man who walks into a club and sees a woman who catches his attention with her dance moves. He invites her to dance with him, and they proceed to do "the butt" dance, which involves shaking their behinds to the beat of the music. The lyrics also mention several women with big behinds and how attractive they are to the singer.


    The song is a celebration of the butt, which was a popular theme in many R&B and hip-hop songs of the time. However, "Da Butt" stands out because of the catchy beat, infectious energy, and humorous lyrics. The song became a massive hit, reaching the top 40 of the Billboard Hot 100 chart and becoming a popular dance anthem in clubs across the country. The success of the song also helped to popularize the dance style, and "the butt" remains a ubiquitous dance move to this day.
