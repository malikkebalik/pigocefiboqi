    Ryan McCartan is Jimmy Award-winning American actor and as well as a singer. He came to prominence after starring in the Disney Channel sitcom Liv and Maddie as Diggie from 2013 until  2017.

    He is also known for his role Jason J.D in the original off-Broadway, Heathers: The Musical in 2014. Apart from acting, he is former members of the pop band The Girl and the Dreamcatcher. Today we are exploring the exciting details about him. Stick with us.

    Dating a 25 Years old Girlfriend, Samantha Fekete Since 2017

    Yes, the 27 years old handsome actor Ryan has someone special in his life. As of now, he is in a healthy love relationship with his new girlfriend, Samantha Fekete, who is a movie actress and YouTuber. She is also a producer at Hi5 Studios.

    McCartan and Fekete started their love life on 13th October 2017. They made their relationship officially through his YouTube video without delay. Ever since they are still going strong and mostly shares each other pictures on their respective Instagram account.

    Former Fiancee-Dove Cameron

    The 6 ft 1 in tall Ryan is an ex-fiancee of American actress or his Liv & Maddie co-star, Dove Cameron, 25. They began dating in August 2013 after sharing the TV screen together. 

    On April 14, 2016, the lovebirds became engaged after three years of a love bond. However, they announced that the couple called off their relationship in October 2016 saying they still love each other very much. 

    Recently in December 2020, the Descendants alum have spilt from her long term boyfriend, Thomas Doherty, after nearly four years of dating. The two first met each other on the set of the film Descendants 2.

    How Much is Ryan McCartan Worth?

    The multitalented American actor and singer, Ryan McCartan has an estimated net worth of $2 Million as of early April 2020. His fortune mainly comes from his successful decade of acting and music career. 

    McCartan is probably best known for acting in the series, Liv and Maddie that aired on Disney Channel from July 19, 2013, to March 24, 2017. It stars Dove, Tenzing Norgay Trainor, Kali Rocha, Lauren Lindsey Donzis, and others. As per his salary, he makes $15k to $60k per episode. His other TV acting credits are Last Man Standing, Royal Pains, Austin & Ally, Midnight, Texas, etc. 

    Moreover, his filmography includes R.L. Stine's Monsterville: Cabinet of Souls, and The F**k Happened. Besides, he and his ex Dove formed the pop duo The Girl and the Dreamcatcher in September 2015 and has 628K subscribers on their YouTube channel as of now. 

    Glowing in the Dark-The Girl and the Dreamcatcher

    Together, the duo released several singles named Written in the Stars, Glowing in the Dark, and Make You Stay. Also, they released their first EP, titled Negatives in May 2016. 

    Bio and Early Life

    Ryan McCartan was born on 14 June 1993 in Excelsior, Minnesota as Ryan Jerome McCartan. He is the son of Conn McCartan (father), a retired principal of Eden Prairie High School and Pam McCartan, a mother. 

    McCartan attended the Minnetonka High School. As per his citizenship, he is American and belong to white ethnicity. In 2007, he started his professional career being the part of Disney's High School Musical.
