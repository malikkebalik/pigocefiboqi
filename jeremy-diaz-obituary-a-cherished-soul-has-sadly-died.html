    Jeremy Diaz Obituary, A Cherished Soul Has Sadly Died

    Jeremy Diaz Obituary, Death It is with profound sadness that we announce the passing of Jeremy Diaz, and during this challenging time, the Williamson County Sheriffs Office extends its heartfelt condolences to the Diaz family.

    27 Jul 2024, 00:00
    0SHARESShareTweet

    Jeremy Diaz Obituary, Death – It is with profound sadness that we announce the passing of Jeremy Diaz, and during this challenging time, the Williamson County Sheriff’s Office extends its heartfelt condolences to the Diaz family.

    On September 11, 2023, our deputies responded to a distressing incident at a residence in the 300 block of Rock Mill Loop, Georgetown. Preliminary investigations revealed that Daniel Logan, a 39-year-old male, forcibly entered a home wielding a baseball bat, resulting in the assault of two individuals, including a six-year-old child. Both victims received immediate medical attention and were transported to the hospital for further care.

    Our team is diligently working on the ongoing investigation, maintaining close collaboration with the District Attorney’s Office. We eagerly await the results of the autopsy conducted by the Travis County Medical Examiner’s Office. Subsequently, if deemed necessary, the Williamson County Sheriff’s Office, in partnership with the District Attorney’s Office, will present the case to a grand jury for potential charges to be escalated to Capital Murder.

    Throughout this process, we pledge unwavering support to the District Attorney’s Office, ensuring all resources are available to aid in the indictment and prosecution of the case. The gravity of this situation underscores the commitment of the Williamson County Sheriff’s Office to justice, seeking accountability for those responsible.

    During this trying period, our thoughts are with the Diaz family, and we implore the community to join us in extending compassion and support to all those affected by this tragic incident.
