    Nicole Fiscella is an American actress and loving mother of three children. She is well known among the public for her role in one of the famous American TV series, Gossip Girl, which was released in 2007, where she portrayed the role of Isabel Coates.

    Fiscella was born on September 15, 1979, at the Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhattan, New York, the United States of America. From an early age, she had a passion for modeling and wanted to become an actress, which she fulfilled later on.
