    Explore Clara Wilsey's Career

    Clara Wilsey has become quite famous as a model and as an actress. She is currently represented by not two but three well-known modeling agencies, "Elite Miami," "NoTies LA," and "Untitled Entertainment."

    In 2019, Clara Wilsey made her acting debut. The HBO TV series "Ballers" marked her onscreen debut. In this show, Clara portrayed Jessica. Even though she was only in one episode, this served as a starting point for her acting career.

She was one of the film's main characters. Clara is portrayed as a mean girl who bullies the protagonist, Jodi due to her being tall.

    "Tall Girl" was a huge hit for Netflix when it launched on the platform on September 13, 2019, garnering an incredible number of views in just four weeks. The film received a mixed reception; it had a 5.2 rating on IMDb and a 38% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes.

    Three years later, in 2022, Netflix released the sequel despite mixed reviews for the film. Kimmy Stitcher, the mean girl from high school, was still played by Wilsey. Following the success of "Tall Girl," Clara went on to star in a short film in 2021 titled "Through April".
