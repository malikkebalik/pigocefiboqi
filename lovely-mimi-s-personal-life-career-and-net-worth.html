    Lovely Mimi has made a name for herself as a Vietnamese-American hip-hop singer, TV reality star, and social media influencer. Her claim to fame includes her appearance on the VH1 reality show Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta in 2017, as well as her reputation as a top nail artist known as the “nail architect.” Additionally, she has amassed a significant following on her social media platforms, where she shares engaging content and captivating photos with her fans.

     
     

    Lovely Mimi Biography & Profile Summary

    Lovely Mimi’s Childhood and Early Life

    Lovely Mimi’s upbringing was marked by hardship, as she spent her early years in a Vietnamese refugee camp before her family resettled in the United States. Growing up in a one-bedroom apartment in Silver Springs, Maryland, Lovely Mimi faced poverty, teasing, and rebellion in her youth. Despite her troubled past, including juvenile detention and dropping out of school, she eventually purchased a nail salon, “Luong’s Lovely Nails,” in Capitol Heights, Maryland.

    Lovely Mimi’s Career and Professional Life

    Lovely Mimi recognized the potential of social media and leveraged platforms like Instagram and YouTube to share her expertise in the beauty industry. Her fame skyrocketed after receiving recognition from celebrities like Da Brat and Snoop Dogg. Additionally, her role in “Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta” allowed her to showcase her life as a reality TV personality and successful nail artist. Currently, she owns five salons in Atlanta.

    Lovely Mimi’s Relationship and Family

    Mimi married Remy Skinner in 2010, and the couple has two children, Juice and Jayy, as well as a dog named Jefe. Remy actively supports Mimi in managing her career and salons.

    Lovely Mimi’s Net Worth

    As of November 2023, Lovely Mimi’s net worth is estimated to exceed $2 million, earned primarily from her nail salons and YouTube channel.

    Social Media Presence

    Mimi has built a significant following on social media, boasting 2.6 million Instagram followers, over 8,400 Twitter followers, and more than 120,000 Facebook followers. Her YouTube channel, “itslovelymimi,” has garnered 500,000 subscribers and nearly 60 million views.
