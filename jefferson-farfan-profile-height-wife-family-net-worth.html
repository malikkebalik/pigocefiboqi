    Jefferson Farfan is an international Peruvian team player. He was born on 26 October 1984 and his birthplace is Lima, Peru. His full name is Jefferson Agustin Farfan Guadalupe.

    Farfan has played in the Alianza Lima, PSV, Schalke 04, Al Jazira, Lokomotiv Moscow club, and the Peru national football team. Generally, his playing position is Forward/Winger. His height is 5 feet 8 1/2 inches.

    Jefferson Farfan club and International career

    In 2001, Farfan started his professional career with Alianza Lima of Peru. In PSV he joined in 2004 and played up to 2008.

    It was reported that PSV had reached an agreement with German club Schalke 04 over the transfer of Farfan for a fee of 10 Million EUR. In fact, Schalke 04 confirmed the transfer of Farfan on a four-year deal on 10 June 2008.

    Afterward, the FC Schalke 04 proclaimed that Farfan had signed a deal extension, which would keep him at S 04 till 30 June 2016.

    Schalke Club confirmed that Farfan had joined Al Jazira Club for a fee of 6 million on a three-year deal on 20 July 2015. After, he signed with a Russian Premier League team FC Lokomotiv Moscow on 26 January 2017.

    In February 2003, Farfan is an important footballer for the Peruvian team, since making his first debut for the national side. Besides, On 23 March 2013, Farfan scored the winning goal in the World Cup qualifier to the Chile team. After, he scored one of two goals in a 2-0 home victory against the New Zealand team that led Peru to qualify for the FIFA World Cup 2018.
