    In this verse, Joey B is reflecting on his past actions and experiences. He admits to having had a lot of money but regrets his choices in women and not realizing the consequences of his actions. He then shares a conversation he had with a wise man who advised him to live differently and not follow the common path of selling his soul for success. Joey B questions the idea of selling his soul and wonders who in their right mind would consider this as a valid option. This verse highlights the struggles of living a fast-paced lifestyle and making choices that ultimately lead to regrets.
