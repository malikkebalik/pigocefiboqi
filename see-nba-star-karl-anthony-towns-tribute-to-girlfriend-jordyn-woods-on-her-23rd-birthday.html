    For long, there have been rumors that NBA star Karl-Anthony Towns and Jordyn Woods were an item. The couple seemed to confirm those rumors on Woods' 23rd birthday.

    Jordyn Woods attends the UOMA Summer House LA at a private residence on August 10, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

    Woods wore a gorgeous black dress with a deep neckline, flaunting her stunning curves. Towns placed his hands on Woods' backside in the picture.

    If that gesture was not significant enough to show the duo is an item, then Towns' caption to the post might have done so. He wrote:

    "Happy BDAY Queen."

    Aside from the picture, Towns also treated Woods to a romantic date to celebrate her birthday, making her feel special like the queen she is. The romantic pair began the day by going quad biking in the desert. After that, they went on a late-night visit to the zoo to see some animals.

    Woods is known as the former best friend of Kylie Jenner and appeared regularly on "Keeping Up With The Kardashians."

    A picture of Karl-Anthony Towns and Jordyn Woods in tribute to her birthday. | Photo: Instagram/Karltowns

    They also had fun, went on a private boat experience, and had dinner. Woods shared a video of herself walking to her birthday dinner in a colorful gown, showing her famous backside.

    Playing in the video's background was the remix of the song, "Said Sum," by Moneybagg Yo, City Girls, and Da Baby. She captioned the post:

    "walking into my birthday dinner like? thank you for all of the sweet wishes ?."

    Woods' birthday is quite special as she turned 23 on September 23. She began celebrations early as she revealed she would be celebrating her new age all week.

    Woods flaunted her gorgeous curves to the delight of her fans, wearing a red plunging T-shirt styled like a crop top. She accessorized with a black hat, floral sliders, and silver jewelry.

    The lavish balloon arch read "Heir Jordan." The model also showed off the bespoke merchandise she made for her birthday celebration which included decorated cups, shot glasses, facemasks, champagne bottles, hand sanitizers, and much more.

    Woods is known as the former best friend of Kylie Jenner and appeared regularly on "Keeping Up With The Kardashians." She is also a model and an Instagram influencer with over 11 million followers.

    Woods is close friends with Will Smith's son, Jaden, who introduced her to Kylie. She is close to the Smiths and refers to Will and Jada Pinkett as uncle and auntie.
