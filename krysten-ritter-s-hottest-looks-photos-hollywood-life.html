    Krysten Ritter is known for being one tough cookie. Check out her hottest red carpet looks right here.

    Krysten Ritter is known for her deadpan sass, dark hair, and badass attitude. The actress, born December 16, 1981, started off as a model before landing several small commercial and TV roles. She rose to prominence on Breaking Bad (2009), before starring on the series Don’t Trust the B**** in Apartment 23 (2012–2013). Next, she dove into the comic book world as Jessica Jones (2015–2019). She reprised the Marvel role in the miniseries The Defenders (2017).

    Other key credits for Krysten include the films What Happens in Vegas (2008), Confessions of a Shopaholic (2009), She’s Out of My League (2010), Veronica Mars (2014), Big Eyes (2014), The Hero (2017), and Nightbooks (2021). With that many credits, she’s become a regular on the red carpet. And every single time, she’s glam. Just like here, during her appearance at the Museum Of Contemporary Art’s 2022 Gala in LA. Her dark hair and black dress looked great with blush and red lipstick.
