    Darby Allin and Sting are two of the most popular and charismatic wrestlers in All Elite Wrestling (AEW). Ever since Sting made his shocking debut in AEW in December 2020, he has been paired up with Darby Allin, forming a mentor-student relationship. The duo has teamed up in several matches, most notably defeating Ricky Starks and Brian Cage in a cinematic street fight at AEW Revolution 2021, and Scorpio Sky and Ethan Page at AEW Double or Nothing 2021.

    But are Darby Allin and Sting related in real life? The answer is no. Despite their similar appearance and style, they are not blood relatives. Darby Allin’s real name is Samuel Ratsch, and he was born on January 7, 1993, in Seattle, Washington. Sting’s real name is Steve Borden, and he was born on March 20, 1959, in Omaha, Nebraska. They have no known family ties or connections.

He also said that Sting is a funny guy and that they like to paint their faces in the car before their matches
